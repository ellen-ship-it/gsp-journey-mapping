Where it breaks — and why

584 people submitted the screening form, then disappeared before reaching registration. Two hypotheses:

UX fatigue: Users see another form after completing screening, and abandon.

Technical: The redirect from form to registration is slow or partially broken.

Either path → kill or rethink the screening-form gate.
Pattern that works — Honeybook: Quick intro questions → personalized welcome video → user is inside the product. Payment comes after experiencing value.
Fullstory confirms (June 8 — Dan): 45 visitors stuck or hesitating at the password step in real time. → Add password-step UX fix to Product sprint list immediately.
